Meenakshi Temple

Description:Meenakshi Amman Temple is a historic Hindu temple dedicated to Goddess Meenakshi (a form of Parvati) and Lord Sundareswarar (Shiva). It’s famous for its towering gopurams, intricate carvings, and vibrant festivals. The temple is a masterpiece of Dravidian architecture.

Location: Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Timings: 5:00 AM – 12:30 PM, 4:00 PM – 9:30 PM

Rating:⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.6/5

Visitor Info for Meenakshi Temple :

  • Cameras and mobile phones must be deposited at the entrance.
  • Evening aarti is a major attraction.
  • Temple closes for 3 hours in the afternoon.

Nearby Places:

  • Thirumalai Nayakar Mahal (1.5 km)
  • Gandhi Memorial Museum (3.5 km)
  • Alagar Kovil (20 km)
