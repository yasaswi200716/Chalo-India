Hampi

Description: Hampi, located in Karnataka, was once the prosperous capital of the Vijayanagara Empire in the 14th century. The site is famous for its captivating ruins, giant boulders, intricately carved temples, and the spiritual energy that flows through every corner of the city. The Virupaksha Temple and Vittala Temple (with its iconic stone chariot) are major highlights.

Location:Hampi, Karnataka

Timings:6:00 AM – 6:00 PM (Open all days)
