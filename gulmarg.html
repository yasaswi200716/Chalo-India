Gulmarg

Description:Gulmarg is a renowned skiing destination with scenic landscapes and one of the highest cable cars in the world — the Gulmarg Gondola. The town transforms into a winter wonderland, offering breathtaking Himalayan views.

Location: Baramulla District, Jammu & Kashmir

Timings: Open all day ; December to March (for snow), April to June (for lush greenery)

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.8/5

Visitor Info for Gulmarg:

  • Ideal for snow sports in winter
  • Gondola ride is a must-do (buy tickets in advance).
  • Snow boots and jackets available for rent.

Nearby Places:

  • Gulmarg Gondola
  • Alpather Lake (13 km)
  • Khilanmarg (6 km)
