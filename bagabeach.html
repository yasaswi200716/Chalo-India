Baga Beach

Description: Baga Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Goa, known for its vibrant nightlife, water sports, beach shacks, and pulsating music. It’s perfect for those looking for a mix of relaxation and entertainment.

Location: North Goa, near Calangute

Timings: Open 24/7 (best visited early morning or evening)

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Visitor Info for Baga Beach:

Activities

  • Jet skiing
  • parasailing, banana boat rides
  • Nightlife at Tito’s Lane
  • Shopping at nearby flea markets
