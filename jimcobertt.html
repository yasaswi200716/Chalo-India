Jim Corbett National Park

Description:Jim Corbett is India's oldest national park and a haven for tiger enthusiasts. With rich flora and fauna, jungle safaris, and riverine landscapes, it’s ideal for nature photographers and adventure seekers.

Location: Nainital District, Uttarakhand

Timings: 6:30 AM – 5:30 PM (varies by zone)

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.5/5

Visitor Info for Jim Corbett National Park:

  • Jeep safaris must be pre-booked.
  • Carry ID proof for entry.
  • Avoid monsoon season (July–October, closed).

Nearby Places:

  • Garjiya Devi Temple (14 km)
  • Corbett Falls (25 km)
  • Kaladhungi Museum (30 km)
