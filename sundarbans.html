Sundarbans National Park

Description:The world’s largest mangrove forest, Sundarbans is home to the elusive Royal Bengal Tiger and saltwater crocodiles. It offers a unique boat safari through dense creeks and mudflats. Lush grasslands, elephants, wild water buffalo, and vibrant birdlife add to its appeal.

Location: West Bengal

Timings: 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM ; Best Time to Visit: September to March

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.6/5

Visitor Info for Sundarbans National Park:

  • Only boat safaris are allowed.
  • Permits required for entry.
  • Carry essentials; remote and limited amenities.

Nearby Places:

  • Sajnekhali Watchtower
  • Dobanki Camp
  • Bonnie Camp (Eco tourism)
