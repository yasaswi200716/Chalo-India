Kumarakom, Kerala

Description: Kumarakom is a tranquil village on the banks of Vembanad Lake in Kerala, famous for its mesmerizing backwaters, luxurious houseboat cruises, and lush greenery. It’s a haven for nature lovers and those seeking peaceful village life experiences.

Location: Kottayam District, Kerala, India

Timings: Open all day
Best Time to Visit: September to March

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.7/5

Visitor Info:

  • Enjoy a serene houseboat cruise through the backwaters.
  • Visit the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary.
  • Try fresh Kerala cuisine with seafood specialties.

Nearby Places:

  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary (3 km)
  • Pathiramanal Island (14 km by boat)
  • Aruvikkuzhi Waterfalls (18 km)
