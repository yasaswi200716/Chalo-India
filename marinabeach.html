Marina Beach – Chennai

Description: Marina Beach is the longest urban beach in India and a cultural landmark of Chennai. Known for sunrise views, street food, and its local buzz.

Location: Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Timings: 5:00 AM – 10:00 PM

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Visitor Info for Marina Beach

Activities

  • Horse rides
  • Try local snacks (sundal, murukku)
  • Visit nearby landmarks like the lighthouse and memorials
