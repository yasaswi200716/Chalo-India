Ooty

Description:Ooty, also known as the “Queen of Hill Stations,” is famous for its tea estates, cool climate, and colonial charm. With serene lakes, gardens, and viewpoints, Ooty offers a peaceful mountain retreat.

Location: Nilgiri Hills, Tamil Nadu

Timings: Open all day ;Best Time to Visit: October to June

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.5/5

Visitor Info for Ooty:

  • Ride the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.
  • Avoid monsoon months (July–September).
  • Local chocolates and tea are must-buys.

Nearby Places:

  • Ooty Lake (2 km)
  • Botanical Gardens (1.5 km)
  • Doddabetta Peak (10 km)
