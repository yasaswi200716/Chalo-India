Varkala Beach – Kerala

Description: Varkala Beach stands out with its dramatic cliffs overlooking the Arabian Sea. Known for a spiritual vibe, it's home to yoga retreats, Ayurvedic spas, and Papanasam beach — where people come for rituals and healing.

Location:Varkala, Kerala

Timings:6:00 AM – 7:00 PM
