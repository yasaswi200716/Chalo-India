Manali

Description:Manali is a picturesque hill town nestled in the Himalayas, loved for its snowy landscapes, pine forests, and adventure sports like skiing, river rafting, and paragliding. It's a perfect escape for both thrill-seekers and nature lovers.

Location: Himachal Pradesh

Timings: Open 24/7 (ideal to explore during daylight hours)

Rating:⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 4.6/5

Visitor Info for Manali:

  • Carry warm clothes, especially in winter.
  • Local taxis available for sightseeing.
  • Rohtang Pass requires a permit (seasonal).

Nearby Places:

  • Solang Valley (13 km)
  • Rohtang Pass (50 km)
  • Hadimba Temple (2 km)
