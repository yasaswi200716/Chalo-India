Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary

Description:Famous for elephants and boat safaris, Periyar offers a mix of wildlife, spice plantations, and scenic lake views in the Western Ghats. It’s a peaceful yet adventurous spot for families and nature lovers.

Location:Thekkady, Kerala

Timings: 6:00 AM – 7:00 PM ; Best Time to Visit: October to March

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.4/5

Visitor Info for Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary:

  • Popular for lake boat rides.
  • Guided forest treks available.
  • Best to book activities in advance.

Nearby Places:

  • Spice Plantation Tours
  • Kumily Market (2 km)
  • Mangala Devi Temple (12 km)
