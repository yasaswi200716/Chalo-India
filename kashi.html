Kashi Vishwanath Temple

Description: Kashi Vishwanath Temple is one of the most revered Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. Located on the banks of the Ganga River, it holds immense spiritual significance and is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas. Pilgrims from across the globe visit to seek blessings and bathe in the sacred Ganges.

Location: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Timings: 3:00 AM – 11:00 PM

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.8/5

Visitor Info for Kashi Vishwanath Temple :

  • Dress modestly (traditional attire preferred).
  • Photography inside the sanctum is prohibited.
  • Early mornings and weekdays are less crowded.

Nearby Places:

  • Dashashwamedh Ghat (0.5 km)
  • Sarnath (10 km)
  • Banaras Hindu University (7 km)
