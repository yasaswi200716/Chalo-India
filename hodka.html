Hodka Village, Gujarat

Description: Hodka is a traditional village in the Kutch region of Gujarat, known for its vibrant local culture, intricate handicrafts, and beautiful mud houses called ‘Bhungas’. It’s a gateway to the White Rann of Kutch and offers a rich experience of local crafts and folk art.

Location: Kutch District, Gujarat, India

Timings: Open all day
Best Time to Visit: November to February (Rann Utsav time)

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.6/5

Visitor Info:

  • Stay at local homestays for a full cultural experience.
  • Shop for Kutchi embroidery, leatherwork, and silver jewelry.
  • Ideal for visiting during the vibrant Rann Utsav festival.

Nearby Places:

  • Great Rann of Kutch (15 km)
  • Bhuj City (60 km)
  • Kala Dungar (Black Hill) (40 km)
