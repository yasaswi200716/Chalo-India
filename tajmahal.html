Taj Mahal

Description: The Taj Mahal, constructed between 1632 and 1653, is an iconic symbol of love and an architectural masterpiece. Commissioned by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal, this UNESCO World Heritage Site combines Persian, Islamic, and Indian architectural styles. It draws millions of visitors every year who come to admire its pristine marble domes, intricate carvings, and perfectly symmetrical gardens.

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Timings: 6:00 AM – 6:30 PM (Closed on Fridays)

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
