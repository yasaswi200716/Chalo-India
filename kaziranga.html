Kaziranga National Park

Description:Home to the world’s largest population of one-horned rhinoceroses, Kaziranga is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Lush grasslands, elephants, wild water buffalo, and vibrant birdlife add to its appeal.

Location: Assam

Timings:7:30 AM – 4:00 PM ; Best Time to Visit: November to April

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.7/5

Visitor Info for Kaziranga National Park:

  • Elephant safaris available early morning.
  • Jeep safari permits needed in advance.
  • Spotting wildlife is easier during early hours.

Nearby Places:

  • Kakochang Waterfalls (13 km)
  • Tea Gardens of Golaghat
  • Orang National Park (120 km)
