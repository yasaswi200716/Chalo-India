Baga Beach

Description: Radhanagar Beach is often ranked as Asia’s best beach, known for its white sand, turquoise waters, and peaceful atmosphere. Ideal for couples, solo travelers, and nature lovers.

Location: Havelock Island (Swaraj Dweep), Andaman & Nicobar Islands

Timings: 6:00 AM – 6:00 PM

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Visitor Info for Radhanagar Beach

Activities

  • Swimming and sunbathing
  • Photography and nature walks
  • Scuba diving and snorkeling (nearby)
