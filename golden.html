Golden Temple (Harmandir Sahib)

Description:The Golden Temple is the holiest Gurdwara and the most important pilgrimage site of Sikhism. Its gold-plated façade, serene water body (Amrit Sarovar), and spiritual ambiance make it one of the most visited and peaceful places in India.

Location: Amritsar, Punjab

Timings: Open 24/7

Rating:⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.9/5

Visitor Info for Golden Temple :

  • Head must be covered inside the premises.
  • Shoes not allowed—must be deposited before entry.
  • Community langar (free meal) is available for all visitors.

Nearby Places:

  • Jallianwala Bagh (300 meters)
  • Partition Museum (400 meters)
  • Wagah Border (30 km)
