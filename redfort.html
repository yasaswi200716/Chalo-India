Red Fort

Description: The Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, was constructed by Emperor Shah Jahan in 1648 as the main residence of the Mughal dynasty. Known for its massive red sandstone walls, intricate Mughal architecture, and cultural significance, it now hosts the annual Independence Day celebrations where the Prime Minister hoists the national flag.

Location: Netaji Subhash Marg, Chandni Chowk, New Delhi

Timings: 7:00 AM – 5:30 PM (Closed on Mondays)

Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
